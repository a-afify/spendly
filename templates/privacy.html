Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter
  • Usage data: pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the Service

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect solely to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To display your expense history and summaries back to you
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve performance

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or financial information with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, your personal information and all associated expense records will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use a limited set of third-party services to help deliver the Service (for example, hosting infrastructure). These providers are contractually required to handle your data only as directed by us and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, data brokers, or analytics platforms that track you across the web.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on our website. We will respond to all reasonable requests within a reasonable timeframe.

You can also review our Terms and Conditions for more information about your rights and responsibilities when using Spendly.
